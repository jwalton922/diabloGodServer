Item Evaluator

DiabloItem.info

A site applying the principals of "big data" analytics to Diablo 3. We are continuously gathering data to provide you with as much interesting information as we can.

To the left is the Item Evaluator. It will show you not only the rank of your item, but information about the heroes that have better and worse items. For instance, you will see average paragon levels, elite kills, progress, and more. Give it a try!

We also have crunched some data and have some general statistics on the hero population by class. You can see the average character stats as well as most common item attributes per slot. Check out the class pages for this info: Barbarian, Demon Hunter, Monk, Witch Doctor, Wizard.

Blizzard released an API a couple of months ago that allows access to a wealth of information about individual player's heroes and account information. We have been collecting that data for awhile now and are creating a site to share the insight we can gain from having that data. The knowledge that can be gleaned from that data is enormous. We can generate statistics about the general Diablo 3 population including things like: the average paragon level, average elite kills, frequency of players upgrading items, how often players play, how much they play, most common attributes per item slot per class, and more. You can check out a beta at www.diabloitem.info. The knowledge in itself is interesting, but it also provides insight into the health of the game, player ranking information, and valuable auction house market information. Selling items on the auction house can be frustrating. It's hard to gauge the worth of an item without a lot of research. Sure, you can look at the auction house at any given time and give it a price relative to those with similar stats, but how often does this work? The price information is handy, but the auction house does not provide you with any information as to the market you are selling to. We can provide you with data about the market an item is valuable too by giving you information about the players who have better items or those who have worse items. Inserting some attributes into our item evaluator will give you information about the population who needs that item on a character basis AND an account basis.
